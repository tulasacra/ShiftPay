About ShiftPay

Scan a crypto payment QR, pay from a BCH wallet.

Supported URI schemes: bitcoin, litecoin, dogecoin, dash, liquidnetwork/liquid, ecash/xec, cardano/web+cardano, algorand/algo, polkadot/dot, ripple/xrp/xrpl, solana/sol, tron/trx.

ShiftPay creates a fixed-rate SideShift shift via the API and then gives you a BCH wallet deep link for the exact deposit. On first launch, ShiftPay creates a SideShift account for this browser; you can replace the keys in Settings or clear them anytime (keys stay on this device only).

Shift history

Stored in this browser only, grouped per SideShift account ID. Tap a shift to re-open it.

    By default, ShiftPay creates an account for you on first launch. To use an existing account from the account page, paste your private key and account ID (affiliate id) here. They never leave your device except when calling SideShift.
